# Contact

This is a sample contact page. The form below is plain HTML markup; it does
not submit anywhere and the dataset does not depend on any contact-form
plugin. Replace it with your own form when you delete the sample content.

<form action="#" method="post" class="contact-form">
  <p>
    <label for="contact-name">Your name</label><br>
    <input type="text" id="contact-name" name="name" required>
  </p>
  <p>
    <label for="contact-email">Your email</label><br>
    <input type="email" id="contact-email" name="email" required>
  </p>
  <p>
    <label for="contact-message">Your message</label><br>
    <textarea id="contact-message" name="message" rows="6" required></textarea>
  </p>
  <p>
    <button type="submit">Send</button>
  </p>
</form>
