# Privacy

This is sample content for theme previews. The privacy policy below is
illustrative only - it describes what an honest, minimal privacy policy
might look like for the kind of small publication this dataset is
imitating. Replace it with a real policy that reflects what your site
actually does before going live.

The dataset itself does not include any tracking pixels, analytics
snippets, third-party fonts, or external embeds whose privacy properties
have not been documented. Every image in `images/` is bundled in the
dataset repository.

## What we collect

If you submit a comment, the following information is stored on the site:

- The name you provided.
- The email address you provided. This is not displayed publicly.
- The website URL you provided, if any.
- The text of your comment.
- The date and time of submission.
- The IP address you submitted from.
- The user-agent string of your browser.

## How we use it

The information is used to display your comment, to assist with moderation,
and to defend against spam. It is not sold, shared, or used for any other
purpose. The IP address is retained for moderation purposes for thirty days
and then deleted.

## Cookies

If you choose to remember your name, email, and URL when commenting, those
values are stored as cookies in your browser. The cookies last for one
year. They contain no other information.

## Embeds

This site uses no third-party embeds. If you add embeds (YouTube, Vimeo,
Twitter, etc) to your version of the site, those services may set their
own cookies and collect their own data. Please document this in your
revised privacy policy.

## Contact

To request a copy of any data the site holds about you, or to request that
it be deleted, write to the address on the [contact page](/contact/).
