A short Kitchen note on cooking in cold weather. The first snowdrop of the
year, photographed in the back garden at half past eight on a Sunday
morning. The snow is the second snow of the season; the first snow vanished
in a day.

We are using the cold snap as an excuse to bring back the winter house
blend on the [Menu](/menu/) and to put the slow-roasted chicken back on for
Friday. Cold weather makes you want to cook things that take a long time.
