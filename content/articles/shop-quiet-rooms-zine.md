A small two-color zine, 24 pages, collecting six short essays from the
Field Notes archive about rooms and the quiet inside them. Letterpress
covers, offset interior, sewn binding.

**What's in it**

- Six essays, lightly edited from their original Dispatch versions.
- Three new line drawings.
- An afterword about the studio's north-facing room and why it is the way
  it is.

**Specifications**

- 24 pages, A5, perfect-bound.
- Cover: 220gsm Colorplan in terracotta, two-pass letterpress.
- Interior: 100gsm uncoated cream, offset.
- Run of 200, numbered.
