We are making lemon cake again on Thursday. The lemons are from the same
small grove they were in February, brought up by the woman with the green
truck who is on her way to a market further east and stops at the studio on
the way past.

This time we are using a little less sugar and a little more zest, which
fixes a thing that was bothering me about the last batch — it was sweet but
not very *lemon*, which seems like the wrong direction for a cake that has
"lemon" in the name.

![A single lemon resting on a folded cotton cloth.](lemon.webp)

The recipe is the one in the back of [Quarterly](/project-quarterly-cookbook/),
the small cookbook we made for a friend last winter. It will be on the
[Menu](/menu/) Thursday and Friday, sold by the slice, until it is gone. It
goes well with the house tea.

If you want to know when the next one is, [Tomás](/team/) keeps a short list
and will write you when there is more.
