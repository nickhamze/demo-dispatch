> A quiet room is not a silent room. A silent room is unsettling - you can
> hear your own pulse, and the small sounds of the building cooling and
> warming, and the faint ringing in your ears that is always there but
> usually masked. A quiet room has a low, steady, ignorable hum: a
> refrigerator three rooms away, a fan in the next building, a ventilator
> on the roof. The hum is what makes the room feel safe. The silence is
> what makes it feel haunted.

A note I keep coming back to. I think about it whenever I am in a hotel
room with the air conditioning off.
