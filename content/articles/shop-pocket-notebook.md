A small pocket notebook, sewn binding, undated, in three cover colors. Made
to fit the back pocket of a pair of work trousers without complaining.

**What's in it**

- 96 pages of cream uncoated paper, lightly ruled.
- Sewn binding, lies flat when open.
- A short list of useful conversions on the inside back cover (oven
  temperatures, cup-to-gram, paper sizes), printed in two colors.

**Specifications**

- 90 × 140 mm.
- Cover: 350gsm uncoated card, in terracotta, sage, or deep teal.
- Paper: 90gsm cream, faintly ruled at 6mm.

**Price:** $14, or three for $36.

**Availability:** In stock, all three colors. We restock these every six
weeks; they are part of the small line we make in the back of the studio
and they are not going anywhere.
