A small tin of the kitchen's winter house blend, blended in the back of the
studio. Enough for about thirty pots if you are generous with the leaves
and forty if you are not.

**What's in it**

- Ceylon black tea (single estate).
- A small amount of Assam, for body.
- Cinnamon, lightly toasted before blending.
- Crushed cardamom pods.
- Whole allspice.

That is everything. No flavorings, no oils, no sweeteners, no decoration.
Pleasant tea.

**Specifications**

- 100 grams loose-leaf, in a sealed steel tin.
- Tin is reusable; please bring it back when it is empty and we will refill
  it for half the price.

**Price:** $22, or $11 for a refill in your own tin.

**Availability:** In stock through end of February. We blend in small
batches; if it sells through faster than expected we may pause and resume.

**To brew:** A heaped teaspoon per cup, water just off the boil, four
minutes. No milk, in our opinion. Sugar is up to you.
