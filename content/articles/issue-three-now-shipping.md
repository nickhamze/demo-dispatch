The third issue is back from the printer and shipping starts this week. If
you pre-ordered, your copy is in the first batch and should arrive within
the next ten days. If you did not, the [Shop](/shop/) has copies, while
they last.

This issue is 48 pages, perfect-bound, with a lithograph cover by an artist
we admire and three new pieces of writing — one from Mira on the long blue
of February evenings, a project case study from Daniyal, and a recipe from
Akosua that involves a quince and a great deal of patience. We are pleased
with how it turned out.

There is also a small index at the back of every previous issue's contents,
which several readers asked for. It does not look like much. We hope it is
useful.

Thanks for reading.
