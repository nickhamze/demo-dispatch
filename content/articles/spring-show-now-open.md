The spring show is up in the front gallery. Six pieces, each numbered and
priced, hung against the new sage wall the way Daniyal kept arguing for and
the rest of us kept resisting. He was right. They look better there.

The work is by three people we have wanted to show for a year. Two prints,
two small oils, and two pieces in mixed media that I do not know what to
call. Come and tell me what you think.

The show runs through June 14. The studio is open Wednesday through Sunday,
ten until six, with the gallery accessible during all open hours. The
opening reception is this Friday evening, six until eight, with bread from
the bakery and the house tea from the kitchen.

A short walk-through is on the [Visit](/visit/) page, including how to get
here from the harbor. If you want to write about it, the [Press kit](/press-kit/)
has the artists' bios and three usage-cleared photographs.
