Daniyal is running a half-day letterpress workshop on Saturday, the 12th of
April, from one in the afternoon until five.

Six places, beginners welcome. You will set type by hand, lock up a small
forme, ink the bed, and pull a short run of two-color cards on the proofing
press. By the end of the afternoon you will have ten cards you printed
yourself and a much better understanding of why your favorite typefaces look
the way they do.

**What to bring**

- Comfortable clothes you do not mind getting ink on.
- A small notebook, if you keep one.
- Lunch is not provided but the kitchen is open, and the bread is good.

**Fee:** $80, which covers materials and use of the press. We waive the fee
for one place per workshop, no questions asked — write to Tomás if that is
useful.

To reserve a place, write to the studio via the [Contact](/contact/) page.
First six replies, in order received.
