A short link out: [The grain of the page](https://example.com/grain-of-the-page)
by a friend of the studio, on the surprisingly long history of laid versus
wove paper and why it matters more than you would expect for the way a
book feels in the hand.

It is the kind of essay we wish we had written ourselves and are glad
someone else did. Recommended for anyone who has wondered why some books
feel right and others do not.
